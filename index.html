About Me

Name: Padona Matabaro Jason

Profession: Environment and Natural Resource Management

M. Matabaro holds a Master's degree (BAC+5) in Environmental Science and Natural Resource Management and has distinguished himself through his passionate commitment to research and the sustainable management of natural resources. He is proficient in a wide range of software and tools for the collection, processing, and visualization of spatial and non-spatial data, including Kobo Toolbox, Survey123, ODK, FieldMaps, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Erdas Imagine, ENVI, Maxent, Spatial Lite, Power BI, R, Python, Google Earth Pro, Shiny dashboards and GeoApp.

His expertise also encompasses advanced spatial data visualization software and techniques, such as ArcGIS dashboards, StoryMaps, Tableau, and interactive 3D modeling. He is also skilled in spatial epidemiological modeling techniques using RStudio and Python, utilizing libraries such as EPIcontact, Epidynamics, Epidm, Epimodel, EpiEstim, Epitools, and EpiNoW2.

These skills enable him to seamlessly integrate data collection, analysis, and visualization workflows to address complex challenges in biodiversity conservation, spatial epidemiology, environmental monitoring, and disaster management.
